### ЁЯХМ What is a Madhab?
A **madhab** is not a separate sect or a new religion. All madhabs rely on the **same Quran and Sunnah**, but differ in how scholars interpreted and applied them.

In early Islamic history, Muslims lived across diverse regionsтАФ**Iraq, Medina, Syria, Egypt**тАФwith varying access to hadith, scholars, and cultural norms. When questions arose (e.g., how to raise hands in salah), scholars referred to Quran and Sunnah and gave rulings based on the evidence available to them.
